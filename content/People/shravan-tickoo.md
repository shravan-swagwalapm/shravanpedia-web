> Founder, product manager, teacher, angel investor, and builder — the person this wiki exists for.

## Context

**Shravan Tickoo** (born February 28, 1993) is an Indian product manager, entrepreneur, and educator based in India. He is the founder and CEO of [[rethink-systems]], a community-led SaaS and education platform, and the creator of [[the-swagwala-pm]], a YouTube channel with over 45,000 subscribers dedicated to teaching product management and AI-assisted development. As of April 2026, Tickoo has mentored over 10,000 product managers, invested in more than 20 early-stage startups across five countries, and built a LinkedIn following of 199,000 with over 190 million impressions per year — ranked the best PM creator in India and third worldwide by Favikon.

What distinguishes Tickoo from a traditional PM educator is that he builds production software himself. Not trained as a developer, he ships code using AI-assisted development tools — primarily Claude Code, which he treats as a CTO-level collaborator rather than an autocomplete tool. This approach forms the basis of his teaching philosophy: that product managers should be able to build, not just manage. His trajectory, as he describes it, is "app builder to AI systems builder," and he teaches others to follow the same path.

His spiritual life as an ISKCON devotee deeply informs his work. Prabhupada's teachings are not a side interest but a foundational lens through which he evaluates decisions — a thread that runs through everything from his [[building-philosophy]] to his most personal project, [[prabhupada-ai]].

## Details

### Early life and education

Tickoo grew up in Jammu, a tier-2 city in northern India. By his own account, he was "relentlessly curious from childhood" — reading about Egyptian mythology, music, religion, and mathematics on the family's first computer in 8th grade, while simultaneously preparing for board exams. Relatives avoided inviting him to their homes, worried he would be a bad influence on their children. His great-grandmother joked he had "come to kill me" because of his relentless questioning.

He attended IIT Roorkee from 2011 to 2015, studying Metallurgy in the top 5% of his batch. Throughout college, he volunteered with the National Service Scheme (NSS), teaching mathematics to underprivileged students. The fact that he studied at IIT Roorkee rather than IIT Bombay or Delhi, and in Metallurgy rather than Computer Science, became a source of imposter syndrome that followed him for years — a story he would later tell publicly as a lesson about authenticity. See [[personal-stories]] for the full account.

He was selected as a Young India Fellow through the Ashoka University and University of Pennsylvania collaboration, a program with a less than 2% acceptance rate.

### Career

Before entering the corporate world, Tickoo built a location-tracking app similar to Waze that went viral in Arizona without any marketing spend — his first entrepreneurial venture, circa 2014-2015. This experience validated what would become a lifelong instinct: build something genuinely useful, and the audience finds you.

His corporate career began at **Flipkart** as a Graduate Trainee (2015-2016), followed by a product role at **BlackBuck** (2016-2017) where he won Employee of the Year. He then moved to **Magicbricks** (2017-2018), **Edureka** as Senior Product Manager (2018-2020) where he won Star Performer, **BYJU'S** as Principal Product Manager (2020-2022), and **Bhanzu** as Director of Product Management (2022-2023). He is self-taught in product management, having learned entirely through self-education and on-the-job experience.

In 2024, after nine years as a product operator, he founded [[rethink-systems]] (registered as Naum Infinitium Private Limited). The company was profitable from day one, generating over $150,000 in its first cohort with zero customer acquisition cost — all from organic community. By April 2026, it had crossed $1 million in revenue through zero-to-one startups, with the 8th cohort of its flagship program launching in September 2026.

### Teaching and content

Tickoo launched his YouTube channel [[the-swagwala-pm]] in July 2021 during his time at BYJU'S. The channel teaches product management and AI-assisted building in Hinglish (Hindi-English mix), making technical concepts accessible to his primarily Indian audience. He has also written the [[builders-bible]], a 700+ page free book on engineering and AI, and runs "Learn 90% of Claude Code in 90 minutes" workshop series.

His teaching extends beyond YouTube. He has lectured at IITs, IIMs, IIM Lucknow, ISB, Masters' Union (as a Visiting Master), and NTU Singapore. He has appeared on podcasts including The One Percent Project and The Growth Podcast with Aakash Gupta. The Product Folks recognized him as one of their "100 PMs" — a curated list of top product leaders in India.

His educational approach is distinctive: he weaves Bhagavad Gita wisdom into modern product execution, a combination his students have described as being "duped into learning human values." His Batch 6 graduation produced the statement that moved him most: "This is not a product management program but a program deeply rooted in human values: resilience, persistence, and the principles of the Bhagavad Gita."

### Angel investing

Tickoo has been an active angel investor since December 2020, with over 20 investments across India, the United States, Canada, Pakistan, and Bangladesh. His portfolio spans healthtech, edtech, SaaS, and consumer tech, with named investments including Foreign Admits, Airtribe, Growrk, Maqsad, Go Paani, MDplus, Thekabook, and Cur8. See [[angel-investing]] for full details.

### Honors and recognition

Tickoo has received several honors over his career, including the Uber Bug Bounty ($3,000 reward for identifying a data vulnerability), the K Start Fellowship, Star Performer at Edureka, Employee of the Year at BlackBuck, and recognition by The Product Folks as one of India's top 100 product managers. In 2024, Favikon ranked him the best Product Management creator in India and third worldwide on LinkedIn.

### Building style

Tickoo ships fast and ships often, typically maintaining five to eight active projects simultaneously. He uses Claude Code as his primary development tool, Obsidian as his persistent context store across sessions, and favors doing less and verifying more over optimizing for speed. His design references include Linear and Stripe as aesthetic benchmarks. His [[building-philosophy]] — "do, then report; only what was asked; prove it" — reflects a deliberate approach that prioritizes shipping things that work over moving fast and breaking things.

## Impact

Shravan Tickoo is the central node of this wiki. Every project, person, inspiration, and idea connects back to his journey as a builder-teacher. The career arc from Flipkart graduate trainee to founding Rethink Systems — and the teaching that ran parallel to every role — is the throughline. His story, as he frames it, is proof that "if a boy from Jammu can do this, we can achieve anything."
