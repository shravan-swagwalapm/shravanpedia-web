> From IIT classrooms to NTU Singapore -- the thread that runs through every role.

## Context

Teaching is not a side activity for Shravan -- it is the constant across every career phase. Even at Flipkart, his first job, the instinct was to explain, to mentor, to make complex things accessible. The YouTube channel, the cohort programs, the [[builders-bible]], the guest lectures at institutions across India and internationally -- they are all expressions of the same impulse. This article captures the teaching arc specifically, separate from the projects it lives inside.

## Details

### Formal Teaching and Guest Lectures

Shravan's guest lecture circuit spans some of India's most prestigious institutions and beyond. He serves as a **Visiting Master** at Masters' Union for "Breaking into Product Management." He has delivered guest lectures at multiple **IITs and IIMs** on product management and building, including a notable appearance at **IIM Lucknow's** Inflection Point 4.0 in March 2024, billed as "BizTalk with Shravan Tickoo (The Swag Wala PM)." The **Indian School of Business (ISB)** added him to their guest lecture circuit. Internationally, he has lectured at **NTU Singapore**, extending his reach beyond the Indian PM ecosystem.

On YouTube, he has been an educator since July 2021 through [[the-swagwala-pm]] channel, which has grown to 45K+ subscribers with 15+ live session series covering a full PM curriculum. The cumulative scale is significant: over 10,000 product managers mentored (per rethinksystems.in, April 2026) and more than 300 placed directly into PM roles through mentoring and channel guidance.

### Podcast Appearances

His podcast appearances extend reach beyond YouTube and LinkedIn into the broader PM creator ecosystem. On **The One Percent Project, Episode 31** (September 2021), he discussed first principles thinking, product defensibility through positioning, and engagement as a critical metric, appearing as Principal PM at BYJU'S at the time. On **The Growth Podcast with Aakash Gupta** (October 2024), he shared tactical PM job search strategies and LinkedIn content strategy, positioned as having trained 500+ professionals who landed roles at top tech companies. These appearances represent peer-to-peer recognition from other PM educators, not just student testimonials.

### Recognition

**The Product Folks** selected him for their TPF 100 PMs list, a peer-nominated recognition of India's top 100 product managers. **Favikon** ranked him the #1 PM creator in India and #3 worldwide, metrics he references on LinkedIn and rethinksystems.in.

### Mentoring Roles

Before building Rethink Systems, Shravan served as a product mentor at **BigShyft** (2020-2021) and **upGrad** (2020-2021). His role at **CUR8** (2023-2026) was a hybrid of educator and investor, combining teaching with angel investing in a single position.

### Curriculum Philosophy

The curriculum blends Bhagavad Gita wisdom with modern product execution -- not as decoration or cultural flavoring, but as foundational framework for how to think about purpose, duty, and detachment from outcomes. Delivery is in Hinglish for accessibility to the Indian audience. The [[builders-bible]] serves as the free, comprehensive text at 700+ pages and always growing.

### Cohort Size Philosophy

Shravan holds a hard conviction on cohort sizing: "You cannot get quality education in any cohort greater than 25 people." The cap is non-negotiable, which creates a natural tension with growth ambitions. His solution is a **gurukul-inspired scaling model**: develop 50 mentors who each train 20 educators, scaling through people rather than class size. The quality stays high because the unit of scale is a trained human, not a larger classroom.

### Three Pillars of Growth

From his Medium and Socialboat writing, three pillars underpin professional growth: **passion** as the prerequisite for everything, **prioritization** as the discipline to focus on what matters most, and **feedback receptiveness** as the willingness to hear and act on criticism. He adds a corollary that reveals his spiritual orientation: "People unconcerned with rewards typically achieve ten times more success."

The 10,000+ PMs mentored is the scale proof. The 300+ placed into PM roles is the conversion proof. The recommendations from students confirm that his teaching style -- practical, narrative, first-hand -- is what differentiates him from textbook PMs. And the NSS volunteering at IIT Roorkee, at ages 18-22, proves this is intrinsic, not a growth hack.
