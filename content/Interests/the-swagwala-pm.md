> Shravan's YouTube brand — teaching PMs and non-devs to build with AI tools. Active since July 2021, 45K+ subscribers.

## Context

**@TheSwagWalaPM** is more than a channel — it is the distribution layer for everything Shravan builds and teaches. The name itself is a statement: a PM who actually ships. "Swag" here means the confidence that comes from building, not just managing. The channel launched in **July 2021** during Shravan's time as Principal PM at BYJU'S, and it has since evolved through three distinct phases that mirror his own professional transformation.

## Details

### Channel Stats

As of April 2026, the channel has **45K+ subscribers** (various sources report 38K-45K depending on the date, with growth ongoing). It has been active since July 2021, primarily in **Hindi/Hinglish** with English technical terms. The YouTube handle is @TheSwagWalaPM (Channel ID: UCbygDe8SRwTaEiFwHdoQxgg), with analytics tracked on PLAYBOARD.

### Content Pillars

The channel's content spans several interconnected pillars. **PM fundamentals** — introductions to product management, how to get a first PM job, product thinking for decision-making — form the core curriculum that built the subscriber base. **Product analytics and strategy** covers advanced material for working PMs: analytics, portfolio building, product strategy and vision. **Live case solving** offers real-time product case walkthroughs in a "show your work" format that differentiates from textbook PM channels. **Claude Code workshops**, including the "Learn 90% of Claude Code in 90 minutes" multi-session series, represent the channel's evolution from PM education to AI-builder education. Session 2 covers git worktrees, team collaboration, CLI-to-app connections, and PR review workflows. The [[builders-bible]] serves as the 700+ page long-form companion, and [[shortstack]] is the planned AI-automated YouTube Shorts pipeline — seven videos per week using ElevenLabs voice clone, HeyGen, and Remotion.

### Content Series and Formats

The channel features a **15+ live session series** that serves as a comprehensive product management curriculum from introductory to advanced fundamentals — the backbone of the channel. Recurring content on **PM certifications and degrees** helps aspirants navigate the certification landscape, while practical **PM job search advice** offers the tactical content that originally built the audience. A deliberate content strategy of **meme-integrated teaching** uses humor to make PM concepts stick — this is how the SwagWalaPM brand differentiates from serious or corporate PM educators.

### Audience

The channel serves PMs who want to code, non-devs learning to build real products, the Indian tech community (through [[hinglish-teaching]] explanations), and PM job aspirants — the largest segment. More than 300 have been helped into PM roles, with over 10,000 mentored overall.

### Channel Evolution

The channel has moved through three distinct phases. **Phase 1 (2021-2023)** was pure PM education — breaking into PM, fundamentals, case studies. This built the subscriber base during Shravan's BYJU'S and Bhanzu years. **Phase 2 (2024)** added the [[rethink-systems]] cohort funnel, turning the channel into the top-of-funnel for the $1,000/seat "Mastering PM 2.0" course. **Phase 3 (2025-present)** is the AI-builder pivot — Claude Code workshops, the Builder's Bible, and teaching non-devs to ship production software. The "PM who builds" identity is now fully crystallized.

### External Appearances

The @TheSwagWalaPM brand extends beyond YouTube into podcast appearances and speaking events. **The One Percent Project** (Episode 31, September 2021) featured Shravan on first-principles thinking, product defensibility, and engagement metrics during his BYJU'S era. **The Growth Podcast with Aakash Gupta** (October 2024) covered tactical PM job search, content strategy for LinkedIn and YouTube, and personal branding. **BizTalk at IIM Lucknow** (March 2024) brought the SwagWalaPM brand to the "Inflection Point 4.0" stage. And **TPF 100 PMs** recognized him as one of The Product Folks' curated list of top product leaders in India.

### Tech Stack for Content

The content creation infrastructure includes **ElevenLabs** for Shravan's voice clone (powering ShortStack), **Excalidraw MCP** for system diagrams used in teaching multi-agent architectures, and the **Obsidian vault** that powers the persistent context behind every session.

## Impact

The channel is the public face of Shravan's "builder-teacher" identity. Every project he builds eventually becomes teaching material, creating the feedback loop described in [[content-flywheel]]: build, learn, teach, build better. The channel's evolution mirrors Shravan's own journey — from PM educator to PM-who-builds educator to AI-builder educator. The 300+ PM placements are the proof of concept; the Claude Code workshops are the future direction. The companion podcast appearances with Aakash Gupta and The One Percent Project amplify reach beyond YouTube into the global PM community.
