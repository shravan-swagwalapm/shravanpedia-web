> AI YouTube Shorts engine — 7 videos/week automated for @TheSwagWalaPM.

## Context

**ShortStack** is the content automation dream: take Shravan's ideas and automatically produce YouTube Shorts at scale. It is the infrastructure play behind the [[the-swagwala-pm]] brand — if the channel needs seven shorts a week to stay competitive, a human cannot sustain that pace. An AI pipeline can.

## Details

### Design

The design is complete and awaiting implementation. The system is a six-stage pipeline: ideation, script generation, voice synthesis, video creation, editing, and publishing. **ElevenLabs** provides Shravan's voice clone — the same provider used by [[prabhupada-ai]], but with a completely different voice model. **HeyGen** handles avatar generation, and **Remotion** powers the programmatic video composition that stitches everything together into a finished short.

### Why It Matters

Content is the distribution layer for everything Shravan builds, and Shorts are the highest-leverage format on YouTube right now. The promise of ShortStack is consistency — the channel never goes dark, even when Shravan is deep in a build sprint or traveling for cohort retreats. Automation does not replace the creator's voice; it amplifies it across a format that rewards volume and frequency.

### Status

The design is complete. Implementation is paused, waiting for the right moment to build. This is a deliberate choice — ShortStack is high-leverage but not urgent, and Shravan's current focus is on projects with more immediate impact.

## Impact

ShortStack represents Shravan's meta-thinking about building: do not just create content, build systems that create content. The builder building a builder. When it ships, it will close the loop on the [[content-flywheel]] by automating the distribution layer entirely.
