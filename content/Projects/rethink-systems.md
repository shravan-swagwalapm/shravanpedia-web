> The parent company behind everything — community-led SaaS, education, and the middleware of the startup ecosystem.

## Context

**Rethink Systems** is the umbrella Shravan founded in 2024 after leaving Bhanzu. It is not one product — it is the platform that ties together the teaching, the building, and the community. Every sub-project — [[rethink-dashboard]], [[builders-bible]], [[the-swagwala-pm]], [[prabhupada-ai]] — lives under this roof. The thesis is community-first: build a community, then build products with and for that community.

## Details

### What It Is

Rethink Systems operates as a community-led SaaS and education platform with two distinct arms. The first is **ed-tech** — education platforms, cohort programs, and free resources like the Builder's Bible. The second is **Talent-as-a-Service**, connecting trained professionals with startup opportunities. Shravan describes the company as "the middleware of the startup ecosystem," sitting between the talent and the companies that need it.

The registered company name is **Naum Infinitium Private Limited**, incorporated in February 2024 and based in Bengaluru. It is bootstrapped and unfunded — growth has been entirely organic. An earlier community called "All Things Product" predates or ran alongside the @TheSwagWalaPM branding.

### Scale

As of April 2026, the numbers tell a clear story: **194K+ LinkedIn followers**, **45K+ YouTube subscribers**, and over **$1M in revenue** generated through 0-1 startups. More than **10,000 product managers** have been mentored, up from an earlier figure of 500+. The cohort programs have drawn **1,600+ applications**, and **20+ video testimonials** from graduates provide social proof. Notable alumni have gone on to roles at Google, Amazon, Microsoft, IBM, Boeing, Salesforce, Flipkart, Mastercard, Airtel Payments Bank, Zomato, Stripe, Oracle, Nagarro, Barclays, ADP, and TCS.

Among the most prominent testimonials are **Varun Khanna** (Product Lead at Google, 12+ YOE), **Isha Singal** (Sr. Technical PM at Amazon, 17+ YOE), **Kevin Thomas** (Senior AI PM at IBM, 5+ YOE), **Deepak Poddar** (Director Global PM at Mastercard, 20+ YOE), and **Puneet Mehra** (VP at Barclays, 21+ YOE).

### Flagship Product: Mastering Product Management 2.0

The centerpiece offering is **Mastering Product Management 2.0**, an 8-week live cohort-based course focused on AI-first execution, design thinking, and real-world building. Each week includes co-building sessions, and the curriculum emphasizes first-principles product thinking, AI tool mastery, product decision-making, and practical execution. The course costs $1,000 per participant.

The first cohort attracted 100 participants from Estonia to Croatia and generated **$150K+ in revenue with zero customer acquisition cost** — all from organic community. The 8th cohort is set to launch on September 27, 2026, under the tagline "Built for Everyone."

### Cohort Philosophy

Shravan holds a firm belief: "You cannot get quality education in any cohort greater than 25 people." This hard cap on cohort size drives a **gurukul-inspired scaling model** — develop 50 mentors who each train 20 educators. The strategy is to scale through people, not class size, preserving the intimacy that makes the education transformative.

### Instructor Positioning

The rethinksystems.in website positions Shravan with 10+ years building products across logistics, edtech, and consumer tech. He is a guest lecturer at IIMs, IITs, ISB, and NTU, and an angel investor in 20+ startups.

### Sub-Projects

The Rethink ecosystem includes several interconnected projects: **Rethink Dashboard** for ed-tech platform infrastructure (see [[rethink-dashboard]]), the **Builder's Bible** as the 700+ page free book at build.rethinksystems.in (see [[builders-bible]]), **@TheSwagWalaPM** as the YouTube and LinkedIn teaching brand (see [[the-swagwala-pm]]), and **PrabhupadaAI** as the AI spiritual Q&A application that carries the most personal meaning (see [[prabhupada-ai]]).

## Impact

Rethink Systems is the proof that the [[content-flywheel]] works at scale. Build in public, teach what you learn, attract a community, monetize through cohorts, reinvest into building more. $150K from a first cohort with zero CAC is the clearest evidence that community-first beats marketing-first for Shravan's model. The company is not just a business — it is the living thesis that everything compounds when the system is designed to feed itself.
